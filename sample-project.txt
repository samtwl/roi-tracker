Project: Customer Service Automation Platform

Project Overview:
We are implementing an AI-powered customer service automation platform to reduce response times and improve customer satisfaction. The project involves deploying chatbots, automated ticket routing, and sentiment analysis tools across our customer support channels.

Key Objectives:
- Reduce average response time from 24 hours to 2 hours
- Increase customer satisfaction scores from 3.2 to 4.5
- Decrease support costs by 40%
- Improve agent productivity by 60%

Current Status:
- Phase 1: Chatbot deployment completed (3 months ago)
- Phase 2: Automated routing system in progress (2 months in)
- Phase 3: Sentiment analysis integration planned (next month)

Metrics Tracked:
- Daily active users: 15,000 (target: 20,000)
- Response time: 3.5 hours (target: 2 hours)
- Customer satisfaction: 3.8/5 (target: 4.5/5)
- Cost per ticket: $12 (target: $8)
- Agent productivity: 45% improvement (target: 60%)

Challenges Identified:
- Low user adoption of chatbot features
- Integration issues with legacy systems
- Training gaps for support agents
- Customer resistance to automated responses

Budget: $500,000
Timeline: 12 months
Team Size: 15 people
